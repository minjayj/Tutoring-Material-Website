Tutor's Study Guide: Unit 1 Kinematics

The Big 3 Kinematic Equations (Uniform Acceleration):

  • $v = v_0 + at$ (Use when missing position $x$)
  • $x = x_0 + v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2$ (Use when missing final velocity $v$)
  • $v^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x - x_0)$ (Use when missing time $t$)

Graphing Motion Rules:

  • The slope of a Position vs. Time graph gives Velocity.
  • The slope of a Velocity vs. Time graph gives Acceleration.
  • The area under the curve of a Velocity vs. Time graph gives Displacement ($\Delta x$).
